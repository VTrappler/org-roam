#+title: Expectation-Maximization algorithm

In [[id:8dcedd6a-85dc-4af5-afde-5936cef961d6][Inference]], the EM algorithm is a technique to find the MAP or the
MLE of a statistical model, usually based on a [[id:3bf80b43-d721-4dc6-8ef5-f0c945f8c647][Mixture distribution]].
