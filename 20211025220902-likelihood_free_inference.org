#+title: Likelihood-free inference

Usually, [[id:8dcedd6a-85dc-4af5-afde-5936cef961d6][Bayesian Inference]] require the evaluation of the likelihood
function, which can be computationally expensive.


An example of Likelihood-free inference is [[id:4a5d9fa2-2475-4e3a-bc37-77ae75c40b27][Approximate Bayesian Computation]].
