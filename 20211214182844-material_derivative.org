#+title: Material derivative
#+startup: latexpreview
#+filetags: :CFD:
The material derivative describes the rate of change of a quantity of a material element subjected to space and time dependent velocity field $\mathbf{u}$:

\begin{equation}
\frac{\mathrm{D}y}{\mathrm{D}t} = \frac{\partial y}{\partial t} + \mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla y
\end{equation}
