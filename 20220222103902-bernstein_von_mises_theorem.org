#+title: Bernstein-Von Mises Theorem

The posterior distribution of $\sqrt{n}(\theta - \theta_0)$ (where
$\theta_0$ is the true value), converges in distribution to a normal
distribution of mean 0 and covariance given by the inverse of the [[id:376e898d-36f4-4f8f-96eb-be7d0d8d8b5e][Fisher
Information Matrix]]
