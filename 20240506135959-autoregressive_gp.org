Scalable multioutput GPs:
Let us consider the problem of modelling $M$ outputs $y_{1..M}(x)$ as [[id:e917a64a-41b6-4eac-a0b7-f4a6c0e6e239][Gaussian Processes]].
The probability distribution of $y_{1..M}$ can be factored:
\begin{equation}
p(y_{1..M}(x)) = p(y_1(x)) \cdot p(y_2(x) \mid y_1(x)) \cdots p(y_M(x) \mid y_{1..M-1}(x))
\end{equation}

\begin{align}
y_1(x) &= f_1(x) &\quad f_1 \sim p(f_1) \\
y_2(x) &= f_2(y_1(x), x)  & \quad f_2 \sim p(f_2) \\
\vdots \\
y_M(x) &= f_{M}(y_{1:M-1}(x), x) & \quad f_{M} \sim p(f_M)
\end{align}
