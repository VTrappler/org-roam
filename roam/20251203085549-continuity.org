#+title: Continuity
* Continuity of linear maps
Let $L:V\to W$ be a linear map between metric spaces (see [[id:c753f247-4fd9-45ef-94b2-400bd92fd58c][Vector Spaces]]).
$L$ is said to be continuous, if $L$ is bounded:
There exists $M$, such that for any $x\in V$
$\|T(x)\|\leq M \|x\|$
