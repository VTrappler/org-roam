#+title: Convolutional Neural Networks
#+filetags: :NeuralNetworks:MachineLearning:

Convolutional [[id:7a245cfe-dcaa-47d6-a318-5574fab3b7ac][Neural Network]] are a specialized type of artificial
neural networks that use a mathematical operation called convolution
in place of general matrix multiplication in at least one of their
layers (wikipedia).
