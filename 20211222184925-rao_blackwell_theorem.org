#+title: Rao-Blackwell theorem
#+startup: latexpreview
Rao-Blackwell theorem is an important theorem of [[id:0bf81a71-2733-4c22-8bad-ae65378a66dd][Estimation Theory]].

Given an unbiased estimator $\delta$, and an exhaustive statistic $S$,
then the estimator given by $\mathbb{E}(\delta \mid S)$ has lower
variance than $\delta$.
This variance can then be compared with the [[id:b8a78df4-8d15-4809-ab28-15d4d41069b4][Cram√©r-Rao Bound]].
